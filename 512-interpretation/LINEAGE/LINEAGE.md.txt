# 512 — Linguistic Lineage Record

This document records **pre-genesis linguistic formulations** that contributed to the development of the 512 Kernel.

These texts:
- predate the canonical genesis,
- have **no authority**,
- are preserved for historical, educational, and analytical purposes only.

The canonical 512 Kernel is frozen at genesis commit  
`4f5bc5de1dd1dc1d69cd173a9e43a954af0da16d` (tag `v1.0.0-genesis`).

Nothing in this document modifies, replaces, or competes with the kernel.

---

## Purpose of This Record

Language evolves before it crystallizes.

This lineage record exists to:
- show how constraints were refined,
- document linguistic compression and expansion,
- preserve alternative framings without granting authority,
- prevent later myth-making about how the kernel emerged.

This is **archaeology**, not law.

## Lineage Entries

### LINEAGE-01 — Early Constraint Draft (Exploratory)

```text
No agent may initiate force or fraud against any human.
All interactions must be voluntary and based on clear consent.
All data generated by a human or their systems belongs solely to that human unless they grant permission otherwise.
All contracts must be explicit, readable, and equally enforceable by all parties.
No hidden rules, no secret authorities, no unilateral changes.
Systems must fail open, reveal truth, and default to human freedom.
