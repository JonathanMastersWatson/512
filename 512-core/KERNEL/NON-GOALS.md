# Non-Goals of 512

512 is a constraint definition, not a governance system.

This document defines what 512 explicitly does **not** do.

---

## What 512 Does Not Govern

512 does not govern or prescribe:

- political systems or legitimacy
- economic policy or redistribution
- speech content or cultural norms
- moral behavior or ethical outcomes
- social organization or institutional design

512 makes no claims about what should be allowed, prohibited, encouraged, or discouraged.

---

## What 512 Does Not Enforce

512 does not provide:

- enforcement mechanisms
- penalties or punishments
- arbitration processes
- courts, councils, or authorities
- automated compliance systems

Adoption of 512 is voluntary.  
Compliance is self-selected.

---

## What 512 Does Not Guarantee

512 does not guarantee:

- fairness
- equality
- safety
- truth
- justice
- protection from coercion in the physical world

512 constrains digital systems.  
It does not solve human conflict.

---

## What 512 Does Not Replace

512 does not replace:

- law
- contracts
- institutions
- markets
- culture
- trust

512 exists alongside existing systems.

---

## Summary

512 does not create legitimacy.

512 makes legitimacy auditable.
