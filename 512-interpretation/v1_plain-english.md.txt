VERSION 1 — Plain English (Boardroom)

---

This text is a subordinate interpretive restatement of the 512 Kernel.
It has no independent authority and does not modify the canonical text.

---

A system MUST NOT trick or coerce a person.

A system MUST operate only with clear permission.

A system MUST allow people to leave at any time.

A system MUST show all terms in plain language and enforce them evenly.

A system MUST NOT hide rules or change them without notice.

A system MUST reveal how it works when something breaks and let people decide.

A system MUST either follow these rules fully or not claim compliance.
