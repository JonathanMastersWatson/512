# Institutional Systems (Advanced)

## Scope

This document covers large-scale institutional use cases operating
under the 512 Kernel.

---

## Welfare Eligibility Systems

Problem:
Automated denials without explanation.

Kernel Constraint:
Fail-open and explicit rules.

Outcome:
Eligibility decisions must reference governing rules and allow appeal
or exit.

---

## Automated Fines and Penalties

Problem:
Penalties issued without human-readable justification.

Kernel Constraint:
No hidden authority.

Outcome:
Systems must disclose rule invocation and evidence class.

---

## Public Procurement Algorithms

Problem:
Hidden favoritism through scoring systems.

Kernel Constraint:
Rule visibility.

Outcome:
Scoring logic must be declared or contestable.

---

## Digital Identity Registries

Problem:
Permanent identity without revocation.

Kernel Constraint:
Consent revocable; exit where possible.

Outcome:
Identity use must be scoped, declared, and contestable.
