# 512 for Economists

512 is a legitimacy infrastructure component.

It operates at the constraint layer.

---

## Economic Role

512:

- reduces uncertainty about rule stability
- lowers verification costs
- limits governance arbitrage

It does not allocate resources.

---

## What Changes

512 enables:

- credible commitment without authority
- reduced reliance on institutional trust
- clearer boundary conditions for markets

---

## What Does Not Change

512 does not:

- set prices
- control capital
- enforce contracts
- determine winners

---

## Summary

512 is not a market actor.

512 alters the cost of trust.
