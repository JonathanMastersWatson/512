# FORBIDDEN MUTATIONS — 512

This list defines mutations that are **NOT allowed** in any interpretation, explanation, teaching material, or derivative language referencing the **512 Kernel**.

If **any forbidden mutation appears**, the text **fails review**.

---

## I. AUTHORITY VIOLATIONS (Hard Fail)

❌ Claiming equivalence to the kernel  
❌ Calling an interpretation *official*, *canonical*, *binding*, or *authoritative*  
❌ Replacing, superseding, or “updating” the kernel  
❌ Presenting interpretations as rules rather than descriptions  
❌ Asserting enforcement power outside voluntary adoption  

### Red Flag Phrases

- “This version replaces…”
- “The updated 512 rule…”
- “The authoritative interpretation…”
- “This expands the kernel to include…”

---

## II. RULE ADDITION (Hard Fail)

❌ Adding new rights, duties, or protections  
❌ Introducing enforcement mechanisms  
❌ Adding penalties, punishments, or remedies  
❌ Defining who enforces compliance  
❌ Introducing compliance gradations or scoring  

### Examples of Illegal Additions

- “Systems must provide compensation…”
- “Violators will be penalized…”
- “Compliance is measured on a spectrum…”
- “A governing body shall…”

---

## III. RULE NARROWING OR SOFTENING (Hard Fail)

❌ Weakening **must** into **should** where the kernel is absolute  
❌ Adding qualifiers like *reasonable*, *where possible*, *best effort*  
❌ Introducing exceptions  
❌ Adding contextual escape clauses  

### Disallowed Modifiers

- “When feasible”
- “Unless required by law”
- “In most cases”
- “To the extent practical”

---

## IV. HIDDEN ENFORCEMENT OR POWER CREEP (Hard Fail)

❌ Introducing supervisory roles  
❌ Implicit trust in operators, platforms, or experts  
❌ Requiring third-party approval  
❌ Creating appeal bodies or oversight committees  

### Red Flag Language

- “Trusted authority”
- “Certified operator”
- “Approved reviewer”
- “Regulatory body”

---

## V. IDEOLOGICAL CONTAMINATION (Hard Fail)

❌ Framing the kernel as political doctrine  
❌ Associating it with movements, parties, or ideologies  
❌ Moralizing language beyond the kernel’s scope  
❌ Identity-based framing  

### Disallowed Framing

- “Progressive values”
- “Anti-capitalist”
- “Pro-democracy movement”
- “Social justice enforcement”

---

## VI. TECHNOLOGICAL BINDING (Hard Fail)

❌ Binding the kernel to a specific technology  
❌ Naming platforms, chains, vendors, or stacks  
❌ Implying required infrastructure  

### Forbidden Couplings

- Blockchains  
- AI models  
- Vendors  
- Clouds  
- Jurisdictions  

> These may exist **around** 512, never **inside** it.
