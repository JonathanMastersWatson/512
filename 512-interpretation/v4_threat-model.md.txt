VERSION 4 — Threat Model Framing (Security)
Style: adversarial

---

This text is a subordinate interpretive restatement of the 512 Kernel.
It has no independent authority and does not modify the canonical text.

---

A system MUST NOT coerce or deceive.
Attack prevented: manipulation and dark patterns.

A system MUST require consent.
Attack prevented: silent enrollment.

A system MUST allow exit.
Attack prevented: hostage systems.

A system MUST disclose terms.
Attack prevented: asymmetric enforcement.

A system MUST expose rules.
Attack prevented: invisible privilege.

A system MUST fail open.
Attack prevented: lock-in during outage.

A system MUST be binary in compliance.
Attack prevented: selective obedience.
