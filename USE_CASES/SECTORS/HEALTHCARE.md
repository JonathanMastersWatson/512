# Health and High-Risk Systems

## Scope

This document describes high-impact systems operating under the 512 Kernel.

---

## AI Triage Systems

Problem:
Life-impacting decisions with opaque logic.

Kernel Constraint:
Fail-open to rule paths.

Outcome:
Decisions must disclose governing logic without exposing models.

---

## Biometric and Genomic Data Platforms

Problem:
Irrevocable data capture.

Kernel Constraint:
Consent scope and revocation.

Outcome:
Users must control scope, duration, and exit.

---

## Risk Scoring

Problem:
Unchallengeable risk classifications.

Kernel Constraint:
No hidden rules.

Outcome:
Scoring categories must be visible and contestable.
